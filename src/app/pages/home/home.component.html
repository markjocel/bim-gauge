Assess your company's current BIM Implementation Maturity

BIM gauge is an online assessment tool to help companies and organization assess their current BIM Implementation Maturity. You can generate result within a few minutes and see what areas are need for improvement.

Welcome to BIM Gauge

1. Set your company profile.

To start the assessment, BIM gauge will ask you to enter some general information about the company.

2. Assess your BIM Implementation Maturity

Next, you will be directed to rate your company's BIM Implementation Maturity in specific areas. Each question can have a score of 0 (lowest) to 5 (highest).

As you move the slider button, you will see the corresponding description of that value state. Choose the state that most corrensponds to your actual condition.

Complete this rating for all five fundamental components:
  • BIM Usage & Information Flow - This components will measure the organization's maturity on BIM usage and information management throughout the whole project lifecycle.
  • Strategy (Collaboration and Working Practice) - This component will measure the collaboration strategy used in the project and their working practice.
  • Infrastrature (Tools & Applications) - This component will measure the organization's capability to deliver BIM from their Software, Hardware, and IT Security
  • Organization & Management - This component will measure the maturity of organization's management in implementing BIM in the project and organization.
  • Information Exchange & Data Structure - This component will measure the maturity of information exchange and data structure used in the project.
  • Personnel (Mentality & Culture) - This component will measure the capacity of the Personnel involved in the implementation of BIM in the organization.

2. Assess your BIM Implementation Maturity

Once you have answered all questions, click the "Submit" button to calculate your result. The organization's Maturity level will be shown as a diagram.
